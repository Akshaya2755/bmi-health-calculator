About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a value derived from the weight and height of a person. It is used as a simple method to classify underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity in adults.

Maintaining a healthy BMI helps reduce the risk of diseases such as heart problems, diabetes, and other health conditions.

BMI Categories

Category BMI Range
Underweight Less than 18.5
Normal 18.5 - 24.9
Overweight 25 - 29.9
Obese 30 and above
